About Us

Rusty Browns started out small, in the back of this dry cleaner place. The kitchen area was not clean, but it was there and was a space where donut stuff could happen and be made.

Then, after some time passed and moved on, things started changing slowly. One shop became two, and then more came after that, so the number of shops increased over time. We made a system that works most of the time so that more people in different places could get donuts from us.

The goal we had in mind was always the same: to make people smile and feel good when eating sweet stuff. Donuts are sugary, fried, and hot. People love to eat them because it makes them feel good.

Donuts from Rusty Brown's aren't just food you eat. It is much more than just something to chew. It is a little memory that stays with us. The outside is somewhat crunchy and the inside is soft in a way that's not hard but more like squishy-soft. Some people like them, some people like them a lot, and others like them less but still maybe kind of like them.
